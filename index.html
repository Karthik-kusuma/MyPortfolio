About Me
Math Is My Passion

I’m Karthikeya Kusuma, a dedicated educator with a passion for transforming students’ academic journeys. With a strong foundation in mathematics and an undergraduate degree from an NIT in India, I have guided over 20 high school students worldwide—from the bustling classrooms of Texas, the sunny coasts of North and South Florida, and the rolling hills of West Virginia, to the vibrant cities of Canada and London and the dynamic communities of India.


Through personalized one-on-one mentorship, I have helped students not only master complex mathematical concepts but also build confidence, develop problem-solving skills, and cultivate a lifelong passion for learning. My approach focuses on tailoring lessons to each student’s strengths and challenges, ensuring they achieve academic excellence while gaining a deeper appreciation for the subject.
